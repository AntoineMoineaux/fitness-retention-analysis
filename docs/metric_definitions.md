# Metric Definitions

## Activation Rate
Percentage of users who complete their first workout within
**0â€“2 days after signup**.

## Retention D7
Percentage of users with **at least one workout between day 7 and day 13**
after signup.

## Retention D30
Percentage of users with **at least one workout between day 30 and day 36**
after signup.

## Weekly Active Users (WAU)
Number of distinct users who complete **at least one workout in a given week**.

## Habit Threshold
A user is considered to have formed a habit when completing
**2 or more workouts in the same calendar week**.

## Cohort Retention
Retention measured as the percentage of users in a signup-month cohort
who are active in week *N* after signup.
